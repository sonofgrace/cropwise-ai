# Changelog

## v1.1.0 - 2026-06-29

### Added

- Location-aware climate-smart recommendation tab.
- Open-Meteo historical weather integration.
- Climate feature engineering module.
- Rule-based climate risk scoring.
- Rainfall and temperature trend charts.
- Climate-aware downloadable report.
- Tests for Open-Meteo integration, climate features, and climate risk scoring.

### Changed

- Updated Streamlit app from single-flow interface to two-tab interface.
- Improved project documentation for climate-aware v2 functionality.

### Notes

The location-aware feature is experimental and should be interpreted as a decision-support extension, not final agronomic advice.
